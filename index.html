Welcome to Our Classroom

At Sunshine Kindergarten, we are dedicated to creating a vibrant, nurturing environment where every child can explore, learn, and grow through play, creativity, and imagination.

Weekly Schedule

Sunday - Magic of Colors & Creations

Children dive into painting, clay modeling, and crafts while learning about colors and textures.

Monday - Little Explorers' Outdoor Quest

We embark on exciting quests around the playground, discovering hidden treasures in nature!

Tuesday - Rhythms of the World

Children explore global rhythms and dance to songs from different cultures.

Wednesday - Storytime Safari

We listen to stories and practice simple Arabic letters.

Thursday - Junior Scientists & Fun Experiments

We become junior scientists, experimenting with fun activities like slime-making.
